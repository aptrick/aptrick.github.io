Newspapers for Hillary

November 6, 2016

Over 350 newspaper editorial boards have issued endorsements in this year’s presidential race. A large majority of them has, with varying levels of enthusiasm, endorsed Hillary Clinton.

Donald Trump has received endorsements from 19 papers—none of them among the country’s 50 largest—an exceptionally low number for a major party nominee.
